# Career Quest: Level Up Your Career

## Overview

Career Quest is a gamified platform designed to help you track your career progress, learn new coding skills, and achieve your professional goals. Think of it as an RPG for your career â€“ earn experience points, unlock new skills, and conquer challenges to level up!

## Key Features

*   **Career Tracking:** Define your career goals, break them down into smaller milestones, and track your progress visually.
*   **Skill Development:**  A curated list of coding skills and resources to help you grow your expertise.
*   **Gamified Progress:** Earn experience points (XP) for completing tasks, contributing to projects, and learning new skills. Level up your profile as you progress.
*   **Challenges & Quests:** Tackle coding challenges and real-world projects to gain XP and demonstrate your abilities.
*   **Community & Collaboration:** Connect with other Career Quest users, share your progress, and collaborate on projects.

## Getting Started

1.  **Create an Account:** Sign up for a free account on the Career Quest platform.
2.  **Define Your Quest:** Set your career goals and create a roadmap for achieving them.
3.  **Start Learning:** Explore the skill development resources and begin your coding journey.
4.  **Take on Challenges:**  Accept challenges and quests to earn XP and level up.
5.  **Share Your Progress:**  Connect with the community and share your accomplishments.
