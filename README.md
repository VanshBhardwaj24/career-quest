# Career Quest: Level Up Your Career

**Career Quest** is a gamified platform that helps you track your career growth, develop coding skills, and achieve your professional goals.  
Think of it as a role-playing game for your career — earn experience points, complete challenges, and level up as you progress.

---

## Overview

Career Quest combines structured learning, goal tracking, and community-driven growth into one platform.  
It provides a clear roadmap for professionals who want to build skills, stay motivated, and track their achievements in a measurable way.

---

## Key Features

### Career Tracking
Set your long-term career goals and break them down into smaller, actionable milestones.  
Visualize your progress through interactive dashboards and progress trackers.

### Skill Development
Access curated learning paths and coding resources designed to help you build technical and professional skills at your own pace.

### Gamified Progress
Earn experience points (XP) for completing learning modules, contributing to projects, and meeting your goals.  
As you gain XP, you’ll level up and unlock new milestones and features.

### Challenges and Quests
Participate in coding challenges, team projects, and real-world problem-solving tasks to apply what you’ve learned and earn rewards.

### Community and Collaboration
Connect with other Career Quest users to share insights, seek feedback, and collaborate on open projects.  
Build your professional network while learning from others.

---

## Getting Started

1. **Create an Account** – Sign up on the Career Quest platform.  
2. **Define Your Quest** – Outline your career goals and set up your personalized roadmap.  
3. **Start Learning** – Explore available skill paths and begin your first lessons.  
4. **Take on Challenges** – Complete quests and earn XP to advance your profile.  
5. **Share Progress** – Engage with the community and showcase your growth.
